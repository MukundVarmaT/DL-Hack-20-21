Deep Learning Hackathon

The Analytics Club, CFI IIT-M presents the annual Deep Learning Hackathon to recognize top Machine Learning enthusiasts within the institute to solve challenging problems. The competetion is split into two tracks:
  • Track - 1 (Computer Vision): Deepfake Detection
  • Track - 2 (Natural Language Processing): Abstract Generation using Research Paper Titles
Please visit the corresponding tabs for more information. For queries please visit the FAQ or you could contact us via email - analyticsclubcfi.iitm@gmail.com

Prizes

Top 2 winners from each track will each be awarded $5000 of AWS credits along with certificates, etc.

Deadline

The competetion closes on April 30th 11:59 PM
