Deep Learning Hackathon

The Analytics Club, CFI IIT-M presents the annual Deep Learning Hackathon to recognize top Machine Learning enthusiasts within the institute to solve challenging problems. The competition is split into two tracks:
  • Track - 1 (Computer Vision): Deepfake Detection
  • Track - 2 (Natural Language Processing): Abstract Generation using Research Paper Titles
Please visit the corresponding tabs for more information. For queries please visit the FAQ or you could contact us via email - analyticsclubcfi.iitm@gmail.com. If interested, please make sure to register for this hackathon here

Prizes

Top 2 winners from each track will each be awarded $5000 of AWS credits + cash prizes, along with certificates.

Deadline

The competition closes on April 30th at 11:59 PM

Announcements

  • [19 April 2021 06:00:33 PM IST] For the sake of clarification, in Track 1 you are NOT allowed to use pretrained models. However, pretrained language models CAN be used in Track 2
  • [16 April 2021 02:55:22 PM IST] If using collab, we strongly recommend the participants to directly download the datasets onto their drive via collab (due to faster download speeds > 100MBPS) instead of manually downloading it and then uploading it to drive. Instructions regarding how to do the same is provided in the dataset information for each track.
